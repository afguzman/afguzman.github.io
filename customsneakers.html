Custom Sneaker Work

Dreamer's Custom Shoes

These custom shoes were created for the organization Dreamers of UW-Madison, a UW Madison campus organization dedicated to helping undocumented/DACA students. They feature a Monarch butterfly on the outer side of the shoe and a DREAM gradient on the inner side of the shoe. The shoe used was an all white Air Force 1 sneaker.

Cartoon Custom Shoes

These custom shoes are my repressentatioon of the popular cartoon custom sneaker trend. It consists of blue, black, and grey colors and black detailing across the shoe. The shoe used was an all white Jordan 1 sneaker.
