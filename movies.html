Movie Recomendations

Whiplash

Whiplash, directed by Damien Chazelle, is a movie about a young drummer that enrolls in one of the top music conservatories in the nation where his dream of greatness is mentored by an instructor that will stop at nothing to realize a students potential.

I particularly enjoy this film because of its unique shots during intense moments in the film. This particular moment is when Terence Fletcher (J.K. Simmons) aggressively goes after a student after hearing an out of tune player.
